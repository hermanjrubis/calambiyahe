Calzada is a commuter guide platform envisioned to transform the way people travel across Calamba and nearby cities. It provides route suggestions, fare information, and transport options for jeepneys, buses, P2P, UV Express, and tricycles to help commuters plan their trips with direction, clarity, and movement.

Simply enter your starting point and destination in the search bar, then click the button to begin. The system will generate several route options, showing travel time, fare cost, and walking distance so you can compare and choose the most convenient journey.

The platform provides estimated fares based on available data. Actual fares may vary slightly depending on operator policies, but the goal is to give commuters a clear idea of expected costs before traveling.
